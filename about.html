About Bright Pets

Bright Pets is a non-profit organization dedicated to finding loving homes for abandoned and neglected animals. We believe every animal deserves a chance at a happy life.

We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Adoption counseling
  • Low-cost spay/neuter programs
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Animal training classes

Our mission is to:

  • Reduce pet homelessness
  • Promote responsible pet ownership
  • Improve the lives of animals in our community
